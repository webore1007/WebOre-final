Frequently asked

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines depend on scope and are confirmed in writing before work begins — landing pages move fastest, platforms with logins and dashboards take longer.

Do you handle hosting and domains?

We can advise on hosting and domains, or hand off deploy-ready code to your own team — this is agreed as part of your project scope.

Can you redesign an existing site?

Yes — we review your current site, keep what's working, and rebuild the rest with a clear plan.

What do you need from me to start?

Your goals, any brand assets you already have, and a filled-out project brief — submit one from your dashboard after signing up.
