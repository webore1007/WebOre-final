Payment Policy

Last Updated: 11 July 2026

At WebOre, we are committed to providing transparent and professional payment practices. This Payment Policy outlines the terms governing payments for our web design, web development, and digital services. By purchasing our services, you agree to this Payment Policy.

Purpose

The purpose of this Payment Policy is to clearly explain the payment process, accepted payment methods, invoicing procedures, due dates, and the responsibilities of both WebOre and the client.

Payment Terms

Unless otherwise agreed in writing, payments shall be made according to the project proposal, quotation, or invoice provided by WebOre.

Projects generally follow a milestone-based payment structure.

Standard Payment Schedule

Unless a different arrangement is agreed upon, our standard payment schedule is:

  • 40% Advance Payment – Required before work begins.
  • 40% After Design Approval – Payable after the client approves the website design or first major milestone.
  • 20% Before Final Delivery – Payable before the final website, source files, or project assets are delivered.

WebOre reserves the right to modify payment schedules for specific projects.

Accepted Payment Methods

WebOre may accept payments through:

  • UPI

The available payment methods may change from time to time.

Currency

Unless otherwise specified, all prices, quotations, and invoices are issued in Indian Rupees (INR).

For international clients, payments may be accepted in another mutually agreed currency.

Invoice

Invoices will be issued for each payment milestone or as otherwise agreed.

Clients are responsible for reviewing invoices and ensuring payment is made by the due date.

Late Payments

If payment is not received by the agreed due date:

  • Work may be paused until payment is received.
  • Project delivery timelines may be extended.
  • Access to completed work may be withheld until outstanding payments are cleared.

Project Suspension

WebOre reserves the right to suspend work on any project if:

  • Payment milestones are overdue.
  • Required information from the client has not been provided.
  • The client fails to respond for an extended period.

Work will resume once outstanding issues have been resolved.

Final Delivery

The final website, source code, design files, login credentials, or other project deliverables will generally be released only after full payment has been received, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Taxes

Where applicable, taxes required by law may be added to the invoice.

Clients are responsible for paying any taxes, duties, or fees applicable to their jurisdiction.

Refunds

Refund requests are governed by the WebOre Refund & Cancellation Policy.

Clients should review that policy before purchasing our services.

Chargebacks

Clients agree to contact WebOre first to resolve any payment disputes before initiating a chargeback or payment reversal.

Fraudulent or unjustified chargebacks may result in:

  • Suspension of services
  • Termination of the project
  • Legal action where permitted by applicable law

Price Changes

WebOre reserves the right to update service prices at any time.

Price changes will not affect projects for which a written quotation has already been accepted unless the project scope changes.

Payment Confirmation

Payments will be considered received only after they have been successfully processed and confirmed by the relevant financial institution or payment provider.

Changes to This Policy

WebOre reserves the right to update or modify this Payment Policy at any time.

Any changes become effective immediately upon publication on our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Payment Policy, please contact us.
